Olive oil is better as it has higher MUFA compared to any other oil. What's MUFA?
Monounsaturated fatty acids or MUFA are a healthy type of fat, and including more of this in your daily diet instead of saturated fats and trans fats may bring several health benefits. Olive, nuts and avocados, among others, are rich in MUFA. From lowering your risk of heart disease to improving the function of your blood vessels, MUFA (and PUFA) only brings good news and so does our olive oil!
What is PUFA?
Short for Poly-Unsaturated Fatty Acids, PUFA are essentially fat molecules that have more than one unsaturated carbon bond in the molecule. When consumed in moderation, PUFA are considered good for health.
